Inside the shop

Chrome, leather, hex lights, hot towels.

Clean floors by 9, fresh capes by 10, hot water on for straight razor shaves all day. Two chairs, big round mirrors, a barber who knows your line the minute you sit down.

We are a walk-in shop. First come first served during the day, and we take appointments via phone or DM for bigger time slots.
